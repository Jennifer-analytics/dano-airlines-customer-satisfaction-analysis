# dano-airlines-customer-satisfaction-analysis
Excel dashboard analyzing airline passenger satisfaction and identifying key experience gaps driving dissatisfaction.


## Problem Statement
Customer satisfaction at Dano Airlines dropped below 50%, raising concerns about passenger experience and long-term loyalty.  
This project analyzes feedback from **129,880 airline passengers** to identify the key drivers of dissatisfaction and highlight priority areas for improvement.

An interactive Excel dashboard was developed to explore satisfaction patterns across passenger segments, delays, service quality, comfort, and digital experience.

---

## Dataset
**Source:** Airline passenger satisfaction survey  
**Records:** 129,880 passengers  

**Key variables include:**
- Passenger demographics (age, customer type)
- Travel characteristics (class, travel type, flight distance)
- Delay information (departure and arrival delays)
- Service, comfort, and digital experience ratings
- Overall satisfaction status

---

## Key Metrics
- Overall Satisfaction Rate (%)
- Percentage of Passengers Affected by Delays
- Satisfaction Rate by Travel Type, Class, and Age Group
- Experience Gap Scores (Comfort, Service Quality, Digital Experience)
- Delay Exposure by Satisfaction Group

---

## Analytical Approach
- Cleaned and prepared the dataset in Excel
- Engineered helper columns for age groups, delay categories, and satisfaction flags
- Used pivot tables to calculate satisfaction rates, delay exposure, and average service ratings
- Designed an interactive dashboard combining KPIs, charts, and experience gap analysis

## Key Insights
- Overall satisfaction remains below 50%, confirming a significant performance issue.
- Economy class passengers account for most dissatisfaction, driven by gaps in seat comfort, legroom, and cleanliness.
- Younger passengers (Under 25 and 25â€“40) and older passengers (60+) report lower satisfaction levels.
- Personal travel passengers are significantly less satisfied than business travelers.
- Dissatisfied passengers experience higher exposure to flight delays.
- The largest experience gaps occur in **Online Boarding, In-flight Entertainment, and Seat Comfort**.

---

## Recommendations
- Improve digital self-service experiences by strengthening online booking, boarding, WiFi, and in-flight entertainment.
- Upgrade Economy class comfort through targeted improvements in seating, legroom, and cleanliness.
- Standardize service delivery across check-in, boarding, and in-flight touchpoints.
- Reduce delay-driven dissatisfaction through clearer communication and real-time updates.
- Enhance first-time passenger journeys to improve early impressions and repeat travel.

---

## Conclusion
These data-driven recommendations provide Dano Airlines with a practical and scalable path to restoring customer satisfaction and strengthening passenger loyalty.
